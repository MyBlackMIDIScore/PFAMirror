How do I download Piano From Above?

You can download Piano From Above by clicking on the Download button on the right.

If you are already using Piano From Above this download will upgrade your current version to the latest version and will keep your existing settings.

Once the download has finished, you can run the installer to install Piano From Above.

What is the Viz Mod?

The Viz Mod is an unofficial optimization modification of the program created by khang06.

Its goal is to deliver the best possible performance for Black MIDIs as well as some few extra features:

Tick-based renderer: A custom renderer that decreases or increases the note speed according to the tempo.
Custom splash MIDI: Gives you the ability to change the startup MIDI of Piano From Above.
Drag'n'drop: You can drag and drop MIDIs directly into Piano From Above.
Text markers: Displays the text markers of the MIDI on the top left corner.
