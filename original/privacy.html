Piano From Above Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 16, 2010

Thank you for visiting PianoFromAbove.com ("PFA"), a site presented to you by Brian Pantano ("Brian"). Your privacy is important to me. As such, I provide this privacy statement explaining my online information practices.

Information I collect and how I use it

When placing orders, PFA's e-commerce provider, FastSpring.com, asks that customers supply certain personal data, including name, physical address, email address, and credit card information. Some of this data is transmitted to Brian. This data is used only for the purpose of fulfilling orders or communicating with customers regarding orders.

Neither Brian nor FastSpring.com stores credit card data on their servers.

Cookies

PFA uses Google Analytics to help analyze how users use the site. Google Analytics uses "cookies," which are small text files on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information in an anonymous form. Google Analytics is the only way in which information from cookies is used. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for information regarding Google's use of this data.

Sharing Personal Information

Brian does not and will not share your personal information with third parties.

Links to Third Party Sites

PFA may link to other web sites on the Internet which are not under the control of or maintained by Brian. Such links do not constitute an endorsement by Brian of those other web sites, the content displayed therein, or the persons or entities associated therewith. You acknowledge that Brian is providing these links to you only as a convenience, and you agree that Brian is not responsible for the content of such sites. Your use of these other linked web sites is subject to the respective terms of use and privacy policies located on the linked sites.

Updating Your Information

If you wish to update your personally identifiable information please contact Brian at support@PianoFromAbove.com

Policy Changes

Brian reserves the right to change this privacy policy, and will post any revisions on its website located at www.PianoFromAbove.com. I encourage you to review this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes. Your continued use of this web site will be subject to the then-current privacy policy.

If you have questions or comments regarding Brian's privacy practices you can contact him at privacy@PianoFromAbove.com.
